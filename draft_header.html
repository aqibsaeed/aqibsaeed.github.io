Research Scientist @ Philips Research
Self-Supervised Learning, Human-Centric AI, Sensing, Audio, & On-device ML

I'm a Research Scientist in AI at Philips Research (Healthcare), the Netherlands. My research interests span Machine Learning, Self-Supervision, Federated Learning, Audio, On-device ML, and applications to understand and improve Personal Health.

I received my Ph.D. cum laude from Eindhoven University of Technology in the Interconnected Resource-aware Intelligent Systems (IRIS) group, where I worked with Johan Lukkien and Tanir Ozcelebi. I have also collaborated with researchers while working at Google Brain and the University of Cambridge. Prior to this, I completed MSc. cum laude in Computer Science (specializing in Data Science and Smart Services) at the University of Twente.

I will be joining the Eindhoven University of Technology as an Assistant Professor of On-body Sensing and Edge AI within the Department of Industrial Design starting in March 2023.
